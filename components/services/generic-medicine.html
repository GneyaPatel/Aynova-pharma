Generic Medicine

A generic drug is a pharmaceutical designed to be identical to a brand-name drug that is currently on the market in terms of dosage and safety.

World class manufacturing facilities:

The "Revised Schedule M" established by the Drug Controller (General) India, Ministry of Health, and Government of India contains strict GMP standards that our manufacturing plant complies with. With a lifelong commitment to quality, Ayunova Pharma's Quality Policy includes all aspects of our business, from employing the best raw ingredients to rigorous control at every production and packaging stage.

Highlights of Manufacturing Facility:
1

No wood or asbestos component.

2

Each zone has separate AHUs (Air Handling Units), dehumidification unit and dust extraction systems.

3

Segregation of every critical processing activity in each zone, to avoid cross-contamination.

4

Respective zones, areas and even uniforms marked with specific colors to ensure total segregation.

5

Air environment conditioned in each area with respect to temperature, humidity, filtration, particle counts, etc.

We offer some of the most affordable prices in the business and adhere to the most intricate client needs. Additionally, we offer private labeling options for export and sell our products through third parties. We produce several different types of branded pharmaceutical formulations. To increase our market share, we are eager to work with importers, distributors, and agents.
