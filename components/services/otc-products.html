OTC Products

Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines are drugs you can buy without a prescription.

AyunovaPharma is quickly expanding its product line in this market by expanding its range of over-the-counter medications and establishing a separate, independent division to manage these goods. The company is expanding into other markets, such as cough syrups, nutritional health goods, gastrointestinal products, protein supplements, skin care products, personal care products, etc., following the success of its pain management products. Drug stores, lifestyle spas, health and cosmetic stores, and retail pharmacies are our main markets.
